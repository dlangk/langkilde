The models are all pretty good. How you harness them makes the real difference.

How To Make Use
of AI Agents

Workflow

The New Core Loop

  • 1. Checklist — every step in precise natural language
  • 2. Inputs — specify inputs, outputs, tools; build what's missing
  • 3. Verification — self-verify first; human feedback where needed
  • 4. Edge cases — iterate, make reversible, add guardrails
  • 5. Dry-run — autonomous end-to-end execution
Documentation is code for agents. If it isn't written down, it doesn't exist. Context management is the next wave.
03

Why The Frontier
Is Jagged

Verification Cost

Three Classes of Progress

Automated

Flywheel at machine speed. Unlimited signal.

Math, code, games, proofs

Cheap Human

Spins slowly but affordably. Seconds per judgment.

Image quality, grammar, RLHF

Expensive Human

Flywheel barely spins. Deep context required.

Strategy, design, safety

Better generation makes human feedback more valuable, not less.
Prediction

The Frontier Gets More Jagged

  • Inductive bias mismatch — human and model difficulty measure different things
  • Verification asymmetry — automated domains accelerate; human-judged stay slow
  • Non-stationarity — moving targets reward adaptation over depth
  • Resource rationality — humans win on total system cost for judgment
A large class of problems will remain where a human at 20 watts is more cost-effective than a data center.

Be More Human

The last two centuries trained humans to be like computers. That era is over. The things that make you human are exactly what remains valuable.

Takeaways
Stop being a computer
The value of human-as-computer is going to zero. Push stable work to AI.
Get comfortable not knowing
Your ability to adapt and act on new information is your edge.
Invest in relationships
Who trusts you matters more than what you know.
Improve your BS filter
Fluency no longer signals competency. Demand hard-to-vary explanations.
Create new value
Don't cut costs. Ask what was impossible before.
Be proud of your humanity
